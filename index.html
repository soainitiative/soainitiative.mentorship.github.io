About S.O.A

This organization was founded by Aisha Sylla and Amy Ogange.

S.O.A is a Mentorship Program for Aspiring Healthcare Professionals bridges the gap in access to opportunities, regardless of gender, race, or socioeconomic class. This program connects aspiring healthcare leaders with experienced mentors who guide and inspire them to unlock their full potential. Participants benefit from tailored mentorship, workshops, and resources that foster confidence, skill development, and career growth. Whether you’re mentoring, learning, or hosting workshops, the SOA Initiative is dedicated to creating a supportive community and making a lasting impact in the field of healthcare.
